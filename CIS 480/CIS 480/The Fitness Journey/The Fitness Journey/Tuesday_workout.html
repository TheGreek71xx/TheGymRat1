Tuesday Workout

Full-Body Strength Training

Warm-up:

  • Jumping jacks: 2 minutes

Strength Training:

  1. Squats
    • 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets
  2. Push-Ups
    • 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets
  3. Bent-Over Rows
    • 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets
  4. Plank
    • 3 sets of 30 seconds
    • Rest 1 minute between sets

Cool-down:

Stretching (focus on legs, chest, and back): 5-10 minutes
