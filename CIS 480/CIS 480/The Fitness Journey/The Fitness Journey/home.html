Welcome to Gym Rat!

Gym Rat is a workout app designed to recommend personalized workout plans and help you track your progress. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, our app offers tailored workout routines to meet your fitness goals.

With our user-friendly interface, you can easily discover new workouts, log your exercises, and monitor your achievements. We're here to support you on your fitness journey, so you can achieve the results you desire.
