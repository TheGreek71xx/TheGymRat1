Friday Workout

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Warm-up:

  • Jump rope: 5 minutes

HIIT Workout:

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest. Repeat the circuit 3 times.

  1. Jump Squats
  2. Push-Ups
  3. Mountain Climbers
  4. Burpees
  5. Plank

Cool-down:

Stretching and deep breathing exercises: 5-10 minutes
