Thursday Workout

Cardio and Core Training

Warm-up:

  • Jumping jacks: 5 minutes

Cardio Training:

  1. Running
    • 30 minutes at a moderate pace

Core Training:

  1. Plank
    • 3 sets of 45 seconds
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets
  2. Leg Raises
    • 3 sets of 12 reps
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets
  3. Russian Twists
    • 3 sets of 15 reps (each side)
    • Rest 1-2 minutes between sets

Cool-down:

Stretching (focus on core and legs): 5-10 minutes
